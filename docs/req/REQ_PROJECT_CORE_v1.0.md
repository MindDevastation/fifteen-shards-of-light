# REQ_PROJECT_CORE_v1.0

## Purpose
Define the stable core requirements for the one-time gift build of **Fifteen Shards of Light**.

## Project Summary
A small personal non-commercial 3D puzzle-platformer gift game built in Godot 4.x with GDScript.

## Canon Requirements
- The protagonist concept is a nine-tailed fox.
- A soul sphere is central to the experience.
- The player collects 15 soul shards across 15 short micro-levels.
- Each completed micro-level reveals a short couplet.
- The hidden phrase is: “Алена, я люблю тебя”.
- The ending is a gentle final epilogue.

## Experience Goals
- Finished playable gift version.
- Target playtime: 15–20 minutes.
- Small, coherent, personal, and emotionally gentle.
- Prioritize completion over expansion.

## Technical Requirements
- Engine: Godot 4.x.
- Language: GDScript.
- Target format: local gift build.

## Scope Constraints
The project must not include:
- Combat.
- Inventory.
- Dialogue system.
- Online features.
- Open world.
- Complex AI.
- Complex RPG systems.
- Polished assets before the greybox build works.

## Production Pipeline
1. Stage 0 — GitHub, AI workflow, local environment.
2. Stage 1 — playable greybox skeleton.
3. Stage 2 — model/visual polish.
4. Stage 3 — sound pass.
5. Stage 4 — final gift polish.
6. Stage 5 — gift build.
